GST Slabs in India

India follows a four-tier GST structure: 5% on essential goods and some services, 12% on standard goods, 18% on most services and goods (the most common slab), and 28% on luxury and sin goods. IGST applies for inter-state transactions while CGST + SGST applies for intra-state transactions — each at half the total GST rate.
