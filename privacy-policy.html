Overview

Calculate Your Finance ("we", "our", or "the site") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data. By using this site, you agree to the terms described below.

Information We Do Not Collect

All calculations on Calculate Your Finance run entirely in your browser using JavaScript. No financial data you enter into any calculator — loan amounts, salaries, investment figures, interest rates — is ever transmitted to our servers or stored anywhere. We have no access to the numbers you enter, and we do not retain them in any form.

We do not require you to create an account, log in, or provide any personal information to use any tool on this site.
